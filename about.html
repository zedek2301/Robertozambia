About Me

Roberto Zambia

Born Robert Chindamba Banda, on the 17th December in Chipata, Roberto is the last born in a family of nine. Hailing from a musical family, both parents being active mucians, Roberto picked up musical interest at a tender age. He and elder brother DAVID BANDA also known as GENERAL OZZY are the only professional artists in the family.

Roberto, recorded his first song in Johannesburg, South Africa at a studio owned by Boom Shakalaka member Junior Sokela. Roberto went on to produce Hip-Hop compilation called THE TAKE OFF, which featured artists from across the African continent ion 2004.

In 2007, Roberto offloaded a song titled Operator, which launched his road to stardom, the song was an instant hit, and this was followed by the release of the self-titled album ROBERTO. 2009 saw the release of his second album AKASUBA, which produced the hits SAVE OUR LOVE, DON’T SAY YOU DO, AND AKASUBA. The 2012 album MY NAME IS… is still related as Roberto’s most successful album, boasting a record 5 number 1 songs. LOVE YOU MORE, CHAWAMA, SALAULA, KAWALALA. Roberto went on to release his monster hit single, AMARULAH which later was the title track of his 4th album, the 5th album is SUPERSTAR. A nick name given to Roberto by his late sister.
